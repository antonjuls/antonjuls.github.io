## 10. Sensor and Geometry Disclaimer

EarthRay's "rays" are computed from consumer-grade smartphone sensors (GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, gravity) using publicly documented geodesy and orientation math. The result is **approximate**. Magnetometer error is typically ±5–15°; GPS altitude error is ±10–30 m; gyroscope drift accumulates over time. The app is an interactive AR toy; it is **not a navigation, surveying, scientific measurement, or safety-critical instrument**. Do not use it for navigation, aviation, vehicle operation, or any decision where accuracy matters. **Do not stare at the screen while moving.** Be aware of your surroundings, traffic, and other hazards.
